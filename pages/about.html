About

The group was a (very low volume) Google Groups only discussion group, until Hussain took the initiative to organise a meetup. Consequently, the first meetup was hosted on 12 April 2014 at House4Hack.

Honourary list of first meetup attendees:

  • Frank
  • Hussain
  • Iwan
  • Tobie
  • Walter
  • Missing our latecomer's name (please raise issue on GitHub with your name)

A record of the post that started it all can be found here.

Some interesting facts about group:

  • We are a diverse bunch of people, from very different backgrounds.
  • Somebody thought it was related to Monty Python.
  • This site is built using Pelican and Markdown, as suggested by Tobie.
